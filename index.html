Introducing California's Greatest Cliff's

What is so unique about California cliffs?

California cliffs are one of a kind in the United States where no cliffs are as magnificent and beautiful as them. And California is the only state that have cliffs that fronts all possible environments: sea, river, mountains and canyons. There are four such individual unique cliffs: West Cliff, North Cliff, East Cliff and South Cliff. All offer fantastic overlooking views, recreational areas, camping sites, wildlife watch spots and overnight cabins.

Where are these cliffs?

Located within 100 miles radius from Irvine , California, they can be reached by roads easily. The closets cliff to Irivine is West Cliff, which is the most popular and most visited.
